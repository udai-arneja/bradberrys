Commercial Landlord and Tenant issues

All aspects related to parties in a lease both residential and commercial are Landlord and Tenant matters.

In addition to advising on variety of conveyancing and litigation in relation to property matters, we regularly deal with both contentious and non-contentious aspects in relation to Landlord and Tenant issues.

This includes:

  • Drafting and negotiating leases, licences for use, change use, alteration and assignment and subletting in accordance with the Lease Code
  • Commercial lease renewals including those Section 25 and 26 notices under Part II of Landlord and Tenant Act 1954
  • Issues related to Dilapidations and Defective Premises Act
  • Surrender and Termination of leases including Possession, Forfeiture and Relief from Forfeiture
  • Matters under the Deposit Protection Scheme
  • Possession proceedings and Recovery of rents and service charges

We also specialise in Residential Lease Extensions and Enfranchisement and assist both Landlords and Tenants in such matters regularly. The website for Leasehold Advisory Service can be accessed at lease-advice.org for some invaluable guidance.

At Bradberrys we offer a wide range of services, providing high-quality specialist advice for all our clients. For any further please contact us on enquiries@bradberrys.co.uk
